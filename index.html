Exploring the Popularity of U.S. Presidents through Wikipedia Page Views

Welcome to this interactive exploration of U.S. presidents' popularity over time, as measured by Wikipedia pageviews. In this visualization, you'll see how public interest in each president has fluctuated month by month in a 12 month period. These pageviews can be influenced by a variety of factors, such as historical anniversaries, current events, educational trends, and media mentions.

As you scroll through, each section will focus on a different president, showing the count of page views for each.
